# Hi I'M Bianca :)
I am a maker Based at **Algarve FAB farm** (South of Portugal), who is passionate about building things and understanding how they work. I have a love for digital fabrication and the tech world, and I am currently working on documenting my projects more often to share what I've learned along the way.
### Professional Highlights
- Design and develop custom Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs).
- Operate and calibrate CNC machinery for manufacturing and prototyping.
- Implement rapid prototyping workflows across laser cutting, 3D printing (SLA/FDM), and vinyl cutting.
- Develop Progressive Web Applications (PWAs) with vanilla JavaScript for offline-first performance.
- Collaborate within Fab Labs to document technical processes and exchange maker knowledge.
- ### Technical Expertise
- **Design & CAD/CAM:** Fusion 360, KiCad, Inkscape, Parametric Design.
- **Hardware/Fabrication:** CNC Machining, Laser Cutting, 3D Printing, PCB Soldering (SMT/Through-hole).
- **Embedded & IoT Platforms:** ESP32, ESPHome, Arduino, I2C/SPI protocols.
- **Systems & Integration:** Home Assistant, Git/GitHub, GitLab.
- ### Main Projects
- **[Fab Academy 2024](https://github.com/biancayoung/Group-assignments)**: A 20-week intensive "How to Make (Almost) Anything" course, based on the curriculum from MIT’s Center for Bits and Atoms. This program focuses on bridging the gap between digital design and physical realization, covering rapid prototyping, precision manufacturing, and multi-disciplinary engineering. It's an immersive journey that culminates in a master's level understanding of digital fabrication.
- **[The Cat Feeder](https://fabacademy.org/2024/labs/kamplintfort/students/bianca-guimaraes/index.html)**: The culmination of my Fab Academy journey. This integrated hardware final project features:
  - **CAD & Mechanical Design**: Full assembly design and CAD for the project build in Fusion 360.
  - **Digital Fabrication**: Precision structures created through laser cutting and CNC milling.
  - **Electronics & PCB**: Custom PCB circuit design, milling, and soldering.
  - **Embedded Control**: Programming and wiring all motors, sensors, and electronic components into a cohesive, automated system.
- **[SkinCalm](https://github.com/biancayoung/skin-tracker-app)**: A personal, privacy-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to track eczema flares and treatment. 
  - **Challenge**: Managing topical treatments with complex application schedules and specific instructions was difficult for me to handle.
  - **Personal Tool**: I built this to help me stay consistent with my skin health routine, keeping everything organized and my data private and local.
